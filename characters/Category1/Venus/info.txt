A lover of all things shining and radiant, Venus is not a planet you're going to miss. Extravagant and lively parties are their speciality, and celestials from all over the galaxy have heard their name. But don't assume they are just show. They wield a rapier by day and the motorcycle by night. Often getting up to antics with Mars and Mercury, not even the most mature among them. Their confidence in themselves lets them quickly be avaliable to help others, the words of Venus are warm.
Loves gems and diamonds!
