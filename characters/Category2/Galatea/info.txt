"I will try my best!" says the waitress in the cute little suit! A rather shy, but determined girl who loves the bar atmosphere! A friendly and soft spoken bird who really does try her best! Working to be able to afford something someday... She can often be seen drinking with a certain Secretary bird during her breaks. She gets pretty lively when drunk! Very cute!
