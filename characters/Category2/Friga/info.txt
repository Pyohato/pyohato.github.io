Arisocratic and lovely swing singer...but also the Head dame of Rosse Piume, a criminal organization. Trigger happy rival to Morrigan, she gets her riches from the trade of Opium and other illegal substances. Find her spending most of her time singing at bars and other high-class events. In the eyes of the public, she's known for her beauty and charm.
Design is based on Frigatebird.
