A Bird from a well known family. Carbonara is the rebellious daughter of Pappardelle Gamba, not wanting to follow the pristine and orderly footsteps the family is known for. For this reason, her family bonds aren't quite strong. Carbonara prefers living the exciting life and on the edge, doing dangerous stunts and fighting. Though she isnt completely anti-social, she's very friendly to new people and gets along with others well. She doesn't like others looking at her eyes...
